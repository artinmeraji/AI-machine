Three Gaps That Kill the Pitch

🔌

No Reliability Story

What happens when WiFi dies? When the bot crashes? When a wrong order is sent during rush hour? If you can't answer this, the pitch is dead on arrival.

Fix: Offline mode + fallback SMS
🤯

Too Technical

Owners don't care about LLaMA or terminals. They care if it breaks at 9 AM on a Saturday. Lead with outcomes, not architecture.

Fix: Outcomes first, always
🎪

Too Many Promises

"Predict everything. Run everything." Sounds fake. Sounds like every other pitch. One owner that believes you is worth ten that are impressed.

Fix: 3 outcomes max per pitch

❌ Never say this

"It does your taxes."

Legal problems. Zero trust. Instant credibility loss.

✓ Say this instead

"It automatically organizes your finances, tracks expenses, and prepares clean reports so your accountant or tax software becomes faster, cheaper, and more accurate."
