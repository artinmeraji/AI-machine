# Local AI — Zero to Hundred

A complete guide to building and running local AI from scratch.
No cloud. No subscriptions. No filters. Your hardware, your models, your rules.

---

## What This Is

This repo documents the full process of:
1. Building a PC optimized for local AI
2. Running uncensored LLMs for cybersecurity work
3. Deploying a local AI assistant for a small business (coffee shop)

---

## Who This Is For

- Someone building their first AI machine
- Cybersecurity learners who need an unrestricted local assistant
- Anyone wanting to deploy local AI for a small business

## The Two Projects

### Project 1 — Your Cybersecurity AI Machine
- Budget: $1,200-1,500
- GPU: RTX 3090 24GB
- Runs: 7B to 34B parameter models
- Use: Security research, CTF, exploit scripting, code generation

### Project 2 — Uncle's Coffee Shop AI
- Budget: $150-350
- No GPU needed
- Runs: 3B-7B models on CPU
- Use: Menu Q&A, customer assistant, order helper
